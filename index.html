Lance bridges image/video understanding, generation, and editing in one compact native unified multimodal model.

Architecture
Shared interleaved multimodal sequence, dual-stream MoE, and modality-aware rotary positional encoding (MaPE).
Training
Staged multi-task learning with adaptive data scheduling across understanding, generation, and editing tasks.
Outcome
Lance outperforms existing open-source unified models on image and video generation tasks within a 128-GPU training budget.
